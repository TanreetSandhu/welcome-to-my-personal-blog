Gippy Grewal

Gippy Grewal is an actor, singer, and producer who has established himself as one of the most successful Punjabi music and film industry artistes. Grewal was born Rupinder Singh Grewal; however, he adopted the stage name "Gippy" because it was his childhood nickname. He completed his early education at Nankana Sahib Public School in Kot Gangu Rai, Ludhiana. He went on to earn a degree in Hotel Management from the North India Institute of Hotel Management in Panchkula.
